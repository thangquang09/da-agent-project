# Metric Definitions

## DAU
DAU (Daily Active Users) is the number of unique users active in a calendar day.
Use DAU with retention to avoid over-reading campaign spikes.

## Revenue
Revenue is recognized gross revenue aggregated by day.
Short-term values may shift after payment reconciliation.

## Retention D1
Retention D1 is the percentage of new users returning on day 1 after install/first play.
Interpret trends with cohort context, not only aggregate daily value.
